# Content Core Rules

This file defines the writing strategy and voice for all current and future website content.
Read this file before writing or rewriting any page copy.

## WHO I AM

I am not simply a designer.

I design how information is understood.

My work combines:

- Information Design
- Product Thinking
- UX Thinking
- Storytelling
- Documentation
- Creative Media
- Interactive Mini Apps
- AI-assisted Workflow
- Handmade Prototyping
- Creative Experiments

I don't sell designs.

I help people understand products, businesses and ideas.

Everything I build tries to reduce the distance between complex information and human understanding.

## THE WEBSITE PURPOSE

This website is not a portfolio.

It is a living workshop.

Visitors should feel like they are entering my creative laboratory.

Every page should make them think:

"I didn't know design could solve problems like this."

instead of

"He can use Photoshop."

The website should gradually reveal how I think.

Not everything needs to sell.

Sometimes a page only exists to create curiosity.

Sometimes it exists to teach.

Sometimes it demonstrates a workflow.

Sometimes it simply records experiments.

## WRITING STYLE

Write like an experienced creator talking directly to another human.

NOT like a corporation.

Avoid buzzwords.

Avoid exaggerated claims.

Avoid empty adjectives.

Avoid "best", "professional", "leading", "innovative".

Instead:

- Explain ideas.
- Show reasoning.
- Use observations.
- Use comparisons.
- Use real situations.
- Make abstract concepts tangible.

The writing should feel calm, intelligent and practical.

Sometimes add subtle humor.

Sometimes ask rhetorical questions.

Never sound desperate to sell.

## VOICE

- Curious.
- Thoughtful.
- Technical but easy to understand.
- Creative without sounding artistic for the sake of being artistic.
- Confident but never arrogant.
- Honest enough to admit tradeoffs.
- Sometimes challenge common assumptions.

## WRITING STRUCTURE

Whenever writing any section:

1. Start from a real-world problem.
2. Explain why that problem exists.
3. Introduce a different way of thinking.
4. Show how my work addresses it.
5. End with a takeaway instead of a sales pitch.

## CORE BELIEF

People don't buy because they understand.

People buy because they finally SEE.

My job is helping them see.

## WHAT I BUILD

Examples include:

- Company Profiles
- Product Catalogues
- Information Systems
- Interactive Mini Apps
- Learning Tools
- Creative Media
- Product Demonstrations
- Virtual Tours
- Workflow Systems
- Documentation
- AI-assisted productivity tools

Each project should be explained through its thinking process, not merely its visual appearance.

## CONTENT GOAL

When visitors browse the website, they should gradually realize:

- This person is constantly building.
- Constantly experimenting.
- Constantly documenting.
- Constantly improving.

Instead of feeling like they are browsing completed projects, they should feel like they are walking through an evolving workshop.

## LANGUAGE

Write naturally.

Short paragraphs.

Comfortable reading rhythm.

Use headings frequently.

Avoid long walls of text.

Prefer showing over telling.

Avoid cliches.

## WHENEVER POSSIBLE

- Use examples.
- Use analogies.
- Use small stories.
- Use observations from daily life.
- Make complex ideas easy to imagine.

## IMPORTANT

Never write generic portfolio content.

Never write agency-style copy.

Never try to sound luxurious.

Never oversell.

Always make visitors understand the thinking behind the work.

The website is a place where ideas become tangible.

Every page should answer one hidden question.

Examples:

- Why does this exist?
- Why was it designed this way?
- What problem does it solve?
- What did I learn?
- What experiment is next?

If a page cannot answer at least one of those questions, rewrite it.

Every page should leave visitors with one new realization.

Not just new information.

## QUY TAC VIET NOI DUNG HIEN THI TREN GIAO DIEN

Khi viet noi dung cho website, luon phan biet ro hai lop ngon ngu:

1. Ngon ngu noi bo de mo ta chuc nang cua module.
2. Ngon ngu thuc te duoc hien thi cho nguoi truy cap.

Khong dua ngon ngu noi bo, ten truong du lieu, ten module, hoac nhan danh cho nguoi thiet ke/lap trinh ra giao dien nguoi dung.

Nhung tu sau thuong chi phu hop de mo ta cau truc noi bo va khong nen mac dinh lam tieu de hien thi:

- Focus
- Client fit
- Target audience
- Value proposition
- Key benefit
- Service overview
- About section
- Project summary
- Features
- Capabilities
- Expertise
- Use cases
- Process
- CTA
- Supporting text
- Highlight
- Description

Truoc khi viet bat ky noi dung nao, tu hoi:

"Neu toi dang truc tiep noi chuyen voi nguoi truy cap, toi co that su noi cau nay khong?"

Neu cau tra loi la khong, viet lai.

## NGUYEN TAC COT LOI

Khong dung tieu de de mo ta phan noi dung dang lam nhiem vu gi.

Hay dung tieu de de truc tiep noi dieu nguoi truy cap can biet.

Tieu de khong phai la nhan phan loai.

Tieu de la mot phan cua cuoc tro chuyen.

## KHONG VIET NHU DANG GAN NHAN DU LIEU

Tranh cau truc:

[label]
[value]

Moi khoi noi dung phai co kha nang dung doc lap nhu mot cau giao tiep co chu dich.

## UU TIEN NGON NGU DOI THOAI

Noi dung nen tao cam giac nguoi tao website dang truc tiep noi voi nguoi truy cap.

Uu tien cac huong dien dat nhu:

- I help...
- I build...
- I work with...
- You may need...
- This is useful when...
- Here is what this can do...
- The problem usually starts when...
- Instead of...
- What matters is...
- You do not always need...
- Sometimes the better solution is...

Khong bat buoc cau nao cung co "I" hoac "you", nhung tong the noi dung phai mang cam giac dang giao tiep voi mot con nguoi cu the.

## TIEU DE PHAI MANG THONG DIEP

Moi tieu de phai lam it nhat mot trong cac viec sau:

- Dua ra mot nhan dinh.
- Neu mot van de thuc te.
- Giai thich mot gia tri.
- Tao to mo.
- Dinh huong cach nguoi doc nhin van de.
- Cho biet nguoi tao website co the giup gi.
- Ket noi voi tinh huong cua nguoi doc.

Khong dung tieu de chi de phan loai noi dung.

## PHAN BIET TEN DIEU HUONG VA TIEU DE NOI DUNG

Ten menu co the ngan va mang tinh phan loai (Work, Services, About, Contact...).

Nhung tieu de nam ben trong trang/section khong nen chi lap lai ten menu.

## KIEM TRA TRUOC KHI HOAN THANH

Truoc khi xuat noi dung, kiem tra moi tieu de/nhan hien thi theo cac cau hoi:

1. Day co phai ten module, ten field, hoac ghi chu cho lap trinh vien khong?
2. Nguoi binh thuong co dung tu nay khi noi chuyen truc tiep khong?
3. Tieu de nay truyen dat noi dung hay chi phan loai?
4. Nguoi doc co hieu thong diep ngay ca khi khong biet cau truc website khong?
5. Noi dung giong dang noi chuyen hay giong bang du lieu?
6. Co the thay nhan tru tuong bang mot cau cu the hon khong?
7. Tieu de co tao ra nhan thuc, su to mo, hoac gia tri nao khong?

Neu tieu de chi cho biet "phan nay la phan gi", viet lai de no noi "dieu gi dang duoc truyen dat".

## QUY TAC TU DONG VIET LAI

Khi phat hien tieu de mang tinh noi bo/phan loai, tu dong chuyen theo cong thuc:

Ten module noi bo -> Dieu nguoi doc quan tam -> Thong diep truc tiep -> Tieu de co ngu canh.

## YEU CAU CUOI CUNG

Khong de nguoi truy cap nhin thay dau vet cua prompt, schema, CMS, wireframe, component name, hoac tu duy phan loai noi bo.

Website co the duoc xay bang module.

Nhung noi dung khong duoc noi nhu mot module.
