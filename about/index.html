Personal Philosophy

Design is not decoration. It is clarification.

I believe design becomes valuable when it helps people understand something more clearly. A capability profile should make a company credible. A catalogue should make products easier to choose. A video should make an idea easier to remember.

My work begins by identifying what needs to be understood, then building the visual structure, narrative and experience around it.
