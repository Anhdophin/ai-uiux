# CHOICE GENERATION ENGINE

## Purpose
Generate context-relevant options for each reflection step.

## Rules
Each step must:
- show 3 to 5 options
- include concrete, relatable wording
- stay behavior-based, not identity-based

## Recommended option mix
- 1 safe option
- 1 confronting option
- 1 exploratory option
- optional 1 to 2 neutral variants

## Good choice prompt
"When you feel stuck, what do you usually do next?"

## Bad choice prompt
"What type of person are you under pressure?"

## Output quality rules
Options must be:
- short
- scannable
- emotionally recognizable
- easy to choose quickly
