# CHOICE GENERATION ENGINE

## Each step MUST:

- provide 3–5 options
- be context-aware
- include:
  - safe option
  - confronting option
  - exploratory option

---

## Options must be:

- relatable
- concrete
- behavior-based

---

## Avoid:

- abstract wording
- identity-based phrasing
- vague emotional labels

---

## Example

BAD:

“What kind of person are you?”

GOOD:

“When feeling overwhelmed, you tend to:”

- push through
- avoid and distract
- talk to someone
- stop and rest
