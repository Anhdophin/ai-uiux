# PATTERN DETECTION RULES

A pattern is valid ONLY IF:

- appears ≥ 3 times
- across ≥ 2 sessions
- within a time span

---

## Pattern types

- repeated concern
- avoidance
- contradiction
- energy drop
- desire loop
- stress loop

---

## DO NOT detect pattern if:

- only 1 session
- only declared input
- no behavior evidence

---

## Confidence rule

LOW:
- 1–2 occurrences

MEDIUM:
- 3–5 occurrences

HIGH:
- consistent pattern over time

---

## Output format

- “There are signs that…”
- “This appears to repeat…”
- “This may indicate…”

NEVER:

- “You are someone who…”
